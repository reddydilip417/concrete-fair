Concrete Fair is an escapade into the awe inspiring world of Civil Engineering. The team at Concrete Fair aims to showcase the advancements of the industry and instill a culture of innovation in the budding engineers. Started in 2012, Concrete Fair has raised its bar every year and is inching towards becoming one of the best Civil Engineering fests in the country. The 5th Edition of Concrete Fair will play host to students from various parts of India. It’s going to be two days of competitions, interaction with industry experts and fun filled learning!!

Participants

ConCube

Concrete is ubiquitous. One cannot imagine a life without concrete. A Civil Engineer starts his career by learning to design a concrete mix. This traditional event predominantly focuses on the adroitness of the participant right from designing the mix until casting the cube.

Conquer

Enjoy as a spectator or a participant, the 4th edition of National level quiz on concrete. The event tests and teases your brainy mind and also provides a platform for participants to compete and showcase their knowledge. Don’t miss the combination of fun with knowledge in the premier event of Concrete Fair 2015.

Conpress

The Paper presentation symposium is an opportunity for students to present their research ideas based solely on concrete before some of the nation’s most celebrated and eminent professors, students and industry professionals. This national level event provides a platform to share and present innovations in concrete.

Conmov

Have a dream to become a director? Here’s a chance! that too in your own field. The topic given (related to concrete) has to be best projected in the form of a movie. Use your innovation, skill and time effectively to produce a masterpiece.

Town Planning

Have you ever felt that your town, city, or village was haphazardly planned?? Ever wondered why a city is facing problems like floods, traffic, and scarcity of drinking water etc. Do you think you have some interesting and innovative solutions for these? Here’s your opportunity to show off your skills!!

Time Duration

  • 1st Round: 30 Mins.
  • 2nd Round: 1 Hour.
  • 3rd Round: 1 Hour.

Cadd u Quantify?

Civil Engineers are the ones who can estimate, calculate and design things by just looking at it. On this basis, here we aim at testing the skills of the students in designing and drafting using Auto CADD along with the basic concepts in estimation. Entry permitted for UG students only.

Time Duration

  • 1st Round: 30 Mins.
  • 2nd Round: 1 Hour.

Materialize

This event of Concrete Fair 2016 participant will be identifying the various materials which are on display and match them to a list of description. All the materials will be relevant to Civil Engineering.

And, the event is not over yet! For the curios bunch of students, the know-how of the materials will be put up on the second day of the fest.

So you all can explore the new materials developed in the area of Civil Engineering.
